Scheduling:

The meetings are highly flexible and can be tailored to fit your daily schedule. I am available from 8 am to 5 pm EST every day. Please let me know the times that work best for you, and we can arrange a meeting accordingly.

Materials:

I utilize a variety of textbooks for classes, including Madinah books, Mastering Arabic books, Al-Kitaab, and others. Once we have selected the best type of lessons that suit your needs, I will provide you with all the necessary resources.

How the Lessons are Conducted:

The lessons take place via Zoom meetings, during which I share my computer screen to provide explanations, engage in discussions, and deliver instruction. (Having your video on is not necessary). All you need is an internet connection, and you can join the session either through your phone or computer, preferably the latter.
